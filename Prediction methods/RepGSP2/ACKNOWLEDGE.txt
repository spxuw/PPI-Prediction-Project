The MSCD library is not original to this toolbox, it refers to the paper:
"Graph Wavelets for 
Multiscale Community Mining", Tremblay and Borgnat, in IEEE 
Transactions on Signal Processing, volume 62, issue 20, 
pp. 5227-5239, October 2014.

The library can be downloaded at the author's webpage
http://www.gipsa-lab.fr/~nicolas.tremblay/index.php

We acknowledge the author for the beautiful and useful work!
